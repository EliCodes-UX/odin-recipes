The BEST way to make some chicken

these bois are gonna be some crispy wings

have you seen chicken so CRISPY

what do you need

ps. this recipe will use about 4 lbs of chicken

  • chicken wings (about 4lbs worth)
  • buttermilk
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika
  • oil

now lets get started

  1. Step 1. Make the breading. in a zip-loc bag mix your flour, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined
  2. Step 2. Dredge the chicken. Pour the buttermilk into a bowl, then dip your chicken into the buttermilk until its COMPLETLY soaked. After all the chicken is soaked in buttermilk place your chicken inside of the zip-loc bag and shake well.
  3. Step 3. Next we transfer your chicken on a baking sheet and allow your coating to become a paste-like consistency.
  4. Step 4. Now we gotta fry it like you were fried that one time you wore those beat shoes to homecoming. Do this by pouring your oil in a skillet and turn it up to high. place chicken in the oil until it turns brown, keep an eye on it because after it browns, reduce the heat, cover the skillet and cook for about 30 mins, and once your timer goes off remove the cover and turn that boy back to high and fry until EXTRAcrispy
